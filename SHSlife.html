What do You know about Life in SHS?

Senior Secondary School Education is onw of the most critical stages of teenagers in this life. It is a place where some of them gain a little privacy from parents yet not fully free , they learn new things amd sometimes unlearn what they have been taught in the beginning.

.

Life in the Senior High School can be very daunting and full of challenges. At the end, some come out as successful, some come out changed for the better, some come out with good values whereas others come out with bad attitudes and no respect for humanity.
